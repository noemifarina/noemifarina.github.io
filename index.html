About me

"Every puzzle has an answer!"

My journey has been guided by a passion for finding creative and functional solutions, a passion rooted in my childhood when, at the age of 9, I delved into the world of puzzles and mysteries by playing Professor Layton. That game sparked a desire to solve problems and create meaningful connections, a passion that found its outlet in interface design. Today, I am ready to bring my passion and skills, aiming to create experiences that are not only functional but also memorable. I am excited to continue learning, growing, and collaborating with those who share my vision of user-centered design.

My journey into the world of design began in 2020 when, amidst the global pandemic, I discovered the world of User Experience while watching a livestream on Twitch. Throughout my educational journey I had the privilege of fully immersing myself in the design process and explore the harmonious fusion of UX and UI. The projects I tackled, especially during my master's program, not only enriched my skills, but also fueled my desire to turn challenges into opportunities. I find great satisfaction in transforming "pain points" into strengths, a process that reminds me the excitement of solving complicated puzzles as a child.
